Shiro also called Shiro Wat or Tsebhi Shiro is an East African stew. Its primary ingredient is powdered chickpeas or broad bean meal and often prepared with

ingredients

  • ½ cup oil.
  • ½ cup chickpea flour.
  • 2 medium onions pureed.
  • 1 roma tomato pureed.
  • 4 cloves of garlic chopped.
  • 2 tablespoons niter kibbeh Ethiopian spiced clarified butter.
  • 2 to 2 ½ cups of water.
  • 3 tablespoons berbere spice
